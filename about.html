ABOUT ME

Wesley Tseng is an aspiring front-end developer with an interest in graphic design and game design. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Game Science from the University of California, Irvine. During his academic career, he specialized in Computer Graphics & Vision and pursued a minor in Digital Arts.

Video games and graphic designs started off as a hobby for Wesley, but as time passed and his passion for games and digital graphics grew, he became inspired to design interactive visuals that can connect with people in meaningful, unique ways.
