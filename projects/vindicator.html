Description

Vindicator was a game created during the Senior Capstone course at UCI over the span of two quarters. The game is a 2D side-scrolling platformer that focuses on narration and combat. Players can choose to play with either a keyboard or PS4 controller.

Vindicator is a game where you play as a hunter that lost his memory and wakes up somewhere familiar. Your task is to explore the area and regain your memory by encountering memory sparks. During your adventure, you'll come across power-ups that allow you to traverse the environment quickly and take-down overwhelming foes.

Controls

PS4 Controller (Preferred)
X - Confirm/Jump
Circle - Cancel/Interact
Square - Base Attack
Triangle - Glove Attack (when unlocked)
Left Stick - Move
Down (D-pad) - Use potion (if any)
R1 - Dash (when unlocked)
Down (Left Stick) + Jump (X) - Drop through platform
Option/Start - Pause/Unpause

Keyboard
Spacebar - Confirm/Jump
Enter - Confirm
Esc - Cancel
X - Interact
Z - Base Attack
A - Glove Attack
Arrow Keys - Move
C - Use potion (if any)
Shift - Dash
Down (Arrow Key) + Jump - Drop through platform
P - Pause/Unpause
